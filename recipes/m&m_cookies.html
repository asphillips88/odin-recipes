Ingredients

  • 1 bag of M&M's
  • 3 Cups of Flour
  • 2 Cups of Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tablespoon Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Turn on oven to 350 degrees
  2. Gather Ingredients
  3. Blend M&M's in food processor
  4. Combine Ingredients and place on cookie sheet
  5. Bake for 12 minutes
